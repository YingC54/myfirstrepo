Ying Chen is a UX Researcher living in Vancouver, B.C., Canada

Ying has a background in English and American Literature, and transitioned to UX through her Masters in Library and Information Studies

Background

There is a saying when studying literature that "Context is Everything", and Ying adapts this philosophy when planning UX research studies.

Ying combines that insight with knowledge on people's interactions with systems and databases from her studies on Libraries and Information to dig into user's interaction.

Ying is a hard-working, passionate, and detail-oriented team player that contributes straightforward UX insights to research projects.

Hobbies

Aside from learning about new research methods, Ying likes to play volleyball with friends, build Lego sets, and ski in the winter.

Hopes and Dreams

Ying hopes to get a husky one day and have fun with her dog.

Philosophy

Having a core philosophy, such as adapting context to the research scope, helps Ying to tackle research projects more realistically and take constraints into consideration when analyzing the data.
